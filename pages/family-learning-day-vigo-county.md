# Family Learning Day at Vigo County Public Library

ArchIvory was thrilled to partner with the Vigo County Public Library in Terre Haute, Indiana for their Family Learning Day festival on September 27, 2025. Visitors participated in a soap-carving activity, learning about the material impact of ivory on material history and the team's research efforts. We made a lot of friends and are excited to visit Vigo County again soon!
